The three charts on this tab present price data for a commodity at different levels of aggregation.

The map shows the average of all the price observations across the selected time-period. The line chart represents the monthly average price for each state during the selected time period. The histogram show the observed prices from each location for the entire selected time period for a particular commodity

Hovering over a state would show data on the state and corresponding observations in the histogram and the line chart. The same is true for the line chart.

The three charts together offer a view of the spread, the trend and the overall levels of prices in different states in Nigeria

Volatility

Volatility measures the magnitude of price fluctuations of a commodity using a 12-month rolling window. Each dot represents an observation from a centre

Market segmentation reflects the extent to which, prices in a country for the same commodity move independently of each other across time. Each dot represents a measurement from a centre

Pass-through refers to the long-term relationship between domestic prices and international prices, i.e., how fully and quickly shocks in prices in international markets are transferred to the Nigerian markets. Each dot represents a state

Zones indicate regions: 1 - South-West, 2 - South-South, 3 - South-East, 4 - North-West, 5 - North-Central, 6 - North-East
